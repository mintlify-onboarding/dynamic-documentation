---
title: "Invite Members"
description:
  "You can invite members of your company to your Dynamic organization by
  Navigating to the Admin screen and clicking on the Members tab. "
---

You can then add a wallet address and a name which will add this wallet to your
organization.

Your team members can then go to app.dynamic.xyz and then your team members can
sign up by:

1. Connecting their wallet
2. Completing the onboarding flow
3. After entering their Alias and Email, they will be asked if they want to join
   your organization. They should click "Join" and then they will be logged into
   your organization's dashboard.
